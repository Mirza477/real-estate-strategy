Content Strategy for Commercial Real Estate

Instagram Reels Strategy for Building Authority & Generating Leads

1. Showcase Expertise

Objective: Position yourself as a go-to authority in commercial real estate.

  • “Insider Tips”: Share quick tips on finding the right commercial properties.
  • Market Updates: Explain current trends with engaging visuals.
  • Financial Tips: Simplify concepts like cap rate, NOI, or financing strategies.

2. Client Success Stories

Objective: Build trust by showcasing real-life wins.

  • Share a “Before and After” reel of a property improvement.
  • Highlight testimonials and success stories with visuals.
  • Walkthroughs of deals you’ve closed, showcasing key numbers.

3. Property Showcases

Objective: Highlight your portfolio and attract leads.

  • Film cinematic walkthroughs of available properties.
  • Showcase unique features with dynamic transitions.
  • Compare properties, asking the audience which they prefer.

4. Educational Content

Objective: Teach your audience about commercial real estate investment.

  • “Common Mistakes New Investors Make” using humor and information.
  • Explain leases (e.g., triple-net leases vs. gross leases).
  • Break down real estate vocabulary in simple terms.

5. Day-in-the-Life Content

Objective: Humanize your brand and connect with your audience.

  • Show behind-the-scenes footage of your workday.
  • “How I analyze a commercial property in 60 seconds.”
  • Highlight networking events and meetings.

6. Motivational & Personal Growth Content

Objective: Inspire aspiring investors and entrepreneurs.

  • Share your journey and success story.
  • Use trending sounds to share motivational quotes.
  • Highlight tools, books, or mentors that have shaped your career.
